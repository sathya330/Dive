Beginner’s Guide to Scuba Diving

🌊 What is Scuba Diving?

Scuba diving is an underwater adventure using special gear to breathe and explore the ocean world.

🎓 How to Start

  • Take a basic training course
  • Get certified
  • Book your first dive

🛟 Safety Tips

  • Always dive with a buddy
  • Check your equipment
  • Never hold your breath underwater

🧰 Equipment Needed

Wetsuit, fins, mask, oxygen tank, dive computer
